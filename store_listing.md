### 🇬🇧 English

```
Rutin is a daily health app that works entirely on your device — no internet, no account, no payment required.

Built for anyone with a regular medicine schedule, trying to build healthy habits, or just wanting to drink more water.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
💊 MEDICINE REMINDERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Alarm-grade reminders — hard to miss
• Rings every minute until the dose is logged
• Snooze 1 minute if you're not ready
• Automatic daily repeat schedule
• Monthly adherence log + PDF export

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🌟 DAILY HABITS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Create habits with emoji, day schedule, and reminders
• Stack habits into routines (habit stacking)
• Daily streaks — see how many days in a row you've stayed consistent
• Earn medals when you retire a habit that's become part of your life

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
💧 WATER TRACKING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Daily target based on WHO guidelines
• Automatic reminders within your active window
• Log a glass in one tap

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🌙 SLEEP MODE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Enable sleep mode before bed
• When you wake up, complete a mini game before using your phone
• Makes mornings more intentional, not just reflexive scrolling

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🏥 TREATMENT PROGRAM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Track long-term treatment programs (TB, Typhoid, ARV, etc.)
• See which day you're on and how many days remain
• Export adherence report as PDF

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✦ WHY RUTIN?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✓ Free forever — no paid features, no subscriptions
✓ Offline — all data stays on your phone, no internet needed
✓ No account — no sign-up, just open and use
✓ No ads — no interruptions
✓ Lightweight and fast

Rutin was built by a student in Bandung who needed a medicine reminder that actually works. Hope it works for you too.
```
